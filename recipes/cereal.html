Cereal: The breakfast that is often surreal

Cereal. It's dried grains and sugars in a bowl of milk. Or is it milk with dried grains and sugars in it? This question can never be definitively answered, because of the simple fact that the truth is still out there. I prefer my cereal with orange juice. That is alright.

The simple story put forth by Big Grain that cereal just appeared one day, as if an angry god reached from the heavens saying to eat 3 square meals a day. This is a lie. God did give us cereal, but it was meant to be consumed at any time of day, not just for breakfast. I am here to spread enlightenment and freedom from breakfasts tyranical structure. Join me.

Ingredients

  • 1 Box of Cereal (anything but Raisin Bran)
  • Milk, Water, or Ideally Orange Juice
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Open the box of cereal. There are a variety of ways to do this, go with what feels right.
  2. Pour the desired amount of cereal into a bowl.
  3. Pour milk or water or orange juice into the bowl with the cereal. Be sure to put MORE milk/water/orange juice in than there is cereal.
  4. Add salt to taste.
