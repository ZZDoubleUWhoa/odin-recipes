Bratwurst: The best or the wurst?

Bratwurst are a tasty treat for any summers day. Consisting of a pigs leftover parts ground into a fine paste, the inside of a bratwurst is heavily seasoned to mask it's unsettling origin. This seasoned paste is then inserted into the dried intestine of a, you guessed it, pig. The bratwurst is a masterclass in waste not, want not. It is no wonder then that it endures in popularity through time.

Today we will discuss the proper method for cooking a bratwurst, or brat as they're known by those in the biz. It is a common misconception that the bratwurst is a ready-made food item that can simply be put on a hot grill. The truth is much deeper than that. Fortunately, I have done this a few times and can share my wisdom in cooking these intestinal wonders.
